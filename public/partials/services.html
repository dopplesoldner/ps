Online Content & Existing Data Sets

SentimentSearch can monitor multiple sources on the Internet to capture what people are saying about your product or service. We provide structured results that illustrate the sentiment of your product or service. Sources include articles, blogs, forums, reviews, Facebook and Twitter.

SentimentSearch can also process your organisation's existing data sets. e.g. consumers surveys and other forms of feedback.

Rest API

SentimentSearch can provide entity and sentiment extraction on content posted via our Rest API.

Customisation

SentimentSearch can provide cusomtised natural language processing algorithms and sentiment analysis algorithms based on the type of content. This ensures the results are accurate and contextually relevant.
